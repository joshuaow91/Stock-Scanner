What Is It?

The STRAT Scanner is a powerful tool designed to help traders quickly identify potential trading setups based on the STRAT methodology. It provides insights into different timeframes and scenarios, making it easier for traders to make informed decisions.

What Are The Strat Scenarios?

Inside Bar (1)

The Inside Bar (1) scenario occurs when the current bar's price range is completely within the previous bar's range. This scenario indicates consolidation and potential breakout in either direction.

Directional Bar (2)

The Directional Bar (2) scenario occurs when the current bar breaks either the high or low of the previous bar. This setup suggests continuation in the direction of the breakout.

Outside Bar (3)

The Outside Bar (3) scenario occurs when the current bar's range engulfs the previous bar, breaking both its high and low. This scenario often indicates increased volatility and potential reversal or continuation.

How to Use

  • Select a Timeframe: Use the buttons at the top (5, 15, 30, 60, D) to select the timeframe you want to analyze. The scanner will update the data accordingly.
  • Analyze the Scenarios: The table below displays stock symbols along with their STRAT scenarios for the selected timeframe. The columns C1, C2, and CC represent the three most recent scenarios, helping you identify trends and patterns.
  • Review the Triggers and Targets: The scanner shows the current price along with the triggers and targets for both bullish and bearish scenarios. These values indicate potential entry and exit points based on the STRAT setup.
